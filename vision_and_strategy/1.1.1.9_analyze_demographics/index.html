    1.1.1.9 Analyze demographics [10025]

    Level: IV (Activity) Analyzing statistical data relating to the size, distribution, and composition of relevant populations, as well as their characteristics. Perform quantitative analysis over raw data-sets gathered from well-founded sources such as government census or large, private databases. Consider employing primary research in collecting required statistics. Use comprehensive studies (reports, briefs, and articles) to assist with the analysis, in place of raw data.

    Process Definition

    Purpose
